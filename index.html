Step 1

Combine rice, sugar, salt and rice vinegar. Combine and season to taste.

Step 2

Cook egg in a nonstick pan. Spread the egg into a thin layer until fully cooked.

Step 3

Roll egg sheet cut into thin strips. Set aside.

Step 4

Using a mandolin or knife, thinly slide cucumber. Set aside.

Step 5

Cover work surface with cling wrap. Put approximately 2 tbsp worth of rice on wrap. Dip your fingers in water if rice is sticky.

Step 6

Shape rice using the cling wrap and twist into a tight ball. Unwrap.

Step 7

Place sliced cucumbers on rice ball and re wrap and shape into a ball shape.

Step 8

Garnish with fish roe.

Step 9

To make fish temari sushi, follow step 4, place piece of sashimi on top and wrap with cling wrap like step 5.

Step 10

Garnish with fish roe.

Step 11

To make egg temari sushi, place egg slices on rice ball and shape.

Step 12

And garnish.

Step 13

Other options for temari sushi garnish are nori, furikake, Japanese pickles, sesame seeds, shiso leaves etc.

Step 14

Using the technique from steps 7 and 8, you can create a variety of temari sushi.
